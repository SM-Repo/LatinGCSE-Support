LatinGCSE Support

Welcome to the support page for LatinGCSE, a fully offline companion app for GCSE Latin learners. The app is completely sandboxed on-device, it does not use an internet connection.

How to Report an Issue

  1. Go to our GitHub Issues page.
  2. Click New Issue.
  3. Describe your issue and submit it.

We will review your issue and respond as soon as possible.
