Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-20

LatinGCSE is a fully offline, on‑device app. It does not make network calls and does not require an internet connection to function.

Information We Collect

  • On‑device data only: Any data generated by your use of the app (e.g., preferences or progress, if applicable) is stored locally on your device. It is not transmitted to us.
  • Support communications: If you contact us for support, we will receive your email address and any information you include in your message.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. Because the app is offline and sandboxed, no personal data is sent to our servers.

Analytics and Third‑Party Services

The app does not use analytics, crash reporting, or third‑party SDKs that transmit data.

Data Retention

We retain support emails only as long as necessary to address your request, unless a longer period is required by law.

Your Choices

  • You may choose not to send support emails. However, we may be unable to assist without sufficient details.
  • You can request deletion of support emails you’ve sent us by contacting us.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

Contact

For questions or requests, please contact us via the support page or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
